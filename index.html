The shoe in the image is the Air Jordan 1 Low FlyEase, a variation of the classic Air Jordan 1 Low designed for easier on and off use. Here are its key features:

  • Design: It maintains the iconic silhouette of the Air Jordan 1 Low, featuring a black and white upper with a red swoosh on the side.
  • FlyEase Closure System: This is the defining characteristic of this variation. It incorporates a wraparound zip and two straps around the heel for a comfortable fit. You can loosen or tighten the straps for a customized feel.
  • Material: The upper is crafted from premium leather, providing a luxurious look and feel.
  • Cushioning: The encapsulated Air cushioning in the sole absorbs impact, ensuring comfort with every step.
  • Traction: The rubber outsole delivers reliable traction on various surfaces.
